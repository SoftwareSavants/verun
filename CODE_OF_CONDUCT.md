# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We want Verun to be a welcoming project for everyone, regardless of experience level, background, or identity. We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and harassment-free environment for all contributors.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and considerate in all interactions
- Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the project and community
- Show empathy toward other contributors

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to conduct@software-savants.com. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated. Maintainers who do not follow or enforce this Code of Conduct may be removed from the project team.
